## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Download the App**: Install Nedil AI from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
2. **Sign Up**: 
   - Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
   - Enter your email address
   - Check your email for a 6-digit OTP code
   - Enter the OTP code to verify your email
   - Create a password
   - Enter your first name (required) and last name (optional)
3. **Complete Onboarding**: Follow the setup wizard to configure your language preferences and voice settings

### First Conversation

1. Tap **"Start New Task"** on the home screen
2. Describe what you need help with (e.g., "I need to check into my hotel")
3. Review and confirm the task description
4. Select your native language and the target language
5. Tap **"Start Conversation"** to begin

## How to Use Features

### Starting a Conversation

**Method 1: Voice Input**
1. Tap and hold the microphone button
2. Speak your task description
3. Release when finished
4. The app will transcribe your speech automatically

**Method 2: Text Input**
1. Type your task description in the text field
2. Tap "Continue" to proceed

**Method 3: Quick Actions**
- Use quick action buttons (Hotel, Food, Transport, Health, Shopping, Directions) for common scenarios
- These provide templates you can customize

### During a Conversation

**Your Side (Bottom Half)**
- **Speak Your Language**: Tap the microphone button to speak in your native language
- **Type Commands**: Use the text input to send instructions or corrections
- **Quick Actions**: Use buttons like "Accept", "Ask price", "Thank you" for common responses
- **Intervene**: At any time, you can whisper commands through earphones or type instructions to guide the conversation

**Their Side (Top Half)**
- **Listen to Them**: Tap the microphone button when the other person is speaking
- **View Translations**: See what they said translated into your language
- **Cultural Tips**: Get helpful cultural context when relevant

### Ending a Conversation

1. Tap **"End Conversation"** when finished
2. Review the **Bonding Phrase** (a helpful phrase to remember)
3. View the **Summary** with key outcomes and important information
4. The conversation is automatically saved to your history

### Viewing Conversation History

1. Tap the **"History"** tab at the bottom of the screen
2. Browse your past conversations
3. Tap any conversation to view details, summary, and full transcript
4. Delete conversations by swiping left (iOS) or long-pressing (Android)

### Managing Settings

1. Tap the **"Settings"** tab at the bottom
2. **Language Settings**: Change your native language preference
3. **Voice Settings**: Choose male or female voice for translations
4. **Account**: Update your name or sign out
5. **Audio Quota**: View your remaining audio time and reset date

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is Nedil AI?**  
A: Nedil AI is a real-time voice translation app that helps you communicate across language barriers. It translates both sides of a conversation in real-time using AI-powered speech-to-speech translation.

**Q: How is Nedil AI different from other translation apps?**  
A: Unlike basic translators, Nedil AI:
- Understands your goals and keeps context throughout conversations
- Provides cultural tips and respectful conversation openings
- Allows on-the-fly interventions to guide the conversation
- Generates clear summaries instead of just transcripts

**Q: Is Nedil AI free?**  
A: Nedil AI offers a free tier with 300 seconds (5 minutes) of audio processing per week. Your quota resets automatically every 7 days.

### Using the App

**Q: How do I start a conversation?**  
A: Tap "Start New Task" on the home screen, describe what you need help with, confirm the task, select languages, and tap "Start Conversation".

**Q: How do I use voice input?**  
A: Hold the microphone button to record your voice. Release when done. Make sure microphone permissions are enabled in your device settings.

**Q: Can I type instead of speaking?**  
A: Yes! You can type your task description and use text input during conversations to send commands or corrections.

**Q: How do I guide the conversation?**  
A: During an active conversation, you can:
- Whisper commands through your earphones
- Type instructions in the text input
- Use quick action buttons (Accept, Ask price, Thank you, etc.)

**Q: What languages are supported?**  
A: Nedil AI supports 20+ languages including:
- English, Tamil, Spanish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean
- French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic
- Russian, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish, Dutch, Polish
- Indonesian, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi

See the language picker in the Preparation screen for the complete list.

**Q: Where can I see my conversation history?**  
A: Tap the "History" tab at the bottom of the home screen. You can view, share, or delete past conversations from there.

**Q: What is a bonding phrase?**  
A: A bonding phrase is a helpful phrase in the target language that you can use to connect with the other person. It's shown at the end of each conversation.

### Account & Settings

**Q: How do I change my language preference?**  
A: Go to Settings → Language Settings → Select your native language

**Q: How do I change the voice gender?**  
A: Go to Settings → Voice Settings → Choose Male or Female

**Q: How do I update my name?**  
A: Go to Settings → Account → Update your first name or last name

**Q: How do I sign out?**  
A: Go to Settings → Account → Tap "Sign Out"

**Q: When does my quota reset?**

## Troubleshooting

### Audio & Microphone Issues

**Problem: Microphone not working**  
**Solutions:**
- Check that microphone permissions are enabled in your device settings
- Restart the app
- Check if other apps can use your microphone
- On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Microphone → Enable Nedil AI
- On Android: Settings → Apps → Nedil AI → Permissions → Enable Microphone

**Problem: Audio not playing**  
**Solutions:**
- Check your device volume
- Ensure your device is not in silent/Do Not Disturb mode
- Try using headphones or earphones
- Restart the app

**Problem: Poor audio quality**  
**Solutions:**
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Reduce background noise
- Hold your device closer when speaking
- Use a quiet environment

### Connection Issues

**Problem: "Nedil Server Unreachable" error**  
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data
- Restart the app
- Check if the app is up to date
- Wait a few minutes and try again

**Problem: Slow translation**  
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection speed
- Close other apps using internet
- Try again in a few moments
- Contact support if the issue persists

### App Crashes or Freezing

**Problem: App crashes when opening**  
**Solutions:**
- Close the app completely and reopen it
- Restart your device
- Update the app to the latest version
- Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is saved in the cloud)

**Problem: App freezes during conversation**  
**Solutions:**
- Wait a few seconds for processing
- Tap the screen to wake the app
- Close and reopen the app
- If the issue persists, contact support

### Translation Issues

**Problem: Incorrect translations**  
**Solutions:**
- Speak more clearly
- Reduce background noise
- Try rephrasing your message
- Use the intervention feature to correct the conversation

**Problem: Translation not in the right language**  
**Solutions:**
- Check that you selected the correct target language
- Go to Settings and verify your language preferences
- Start a new conversation with the correct language selection

### Account Issues

**Problem: Can't sign in**  
**Solutions:**
- Verify your email address is correct
- Check your internet connection
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Problem: OTP code not received**  
**Solutions:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address is correct
- Wait a few minutes and request a new code
- Check that your email service is working
- Contact support if you still don't receive the code

**Problem: Forgot password**  
**Solutions:**
- Use the "Forgot Password" option on the sign-in screen
- Check your email for password reset instructions
- Contact support if you need additional help

## Quick Tips

### For Best Results

1. **Speak Clearly**: Enunciate your words and speak at a normal pace
2. **Reduce Noise**: Use the app in a quiet environment when possible
3. **Be Specific**: Provide clear task descriptions for better context
4. **Use Quick Actions**: Save time with quick action buttons for common scenarios
5. **Review Summaries**: Check conversation summaries to remember important details
6. **Save Bonding Phrases**: Note helpful phrases from bonding phrase cards

### Common Use Cases

- **Hotel Check-in**: "I need to check into my hotel. The reservation is under [name]."
- **Restaurant Orders**: "I want to order food. I'm looking for [cuisine type]."
- **Transportation**: "I need transportation. I want to go to [destination]."
- **Medical Assistance**: "I need medical assistance. I'm experiencing [symptoms]."
- **Shopping**: "I want to buy something. I'm looking for [item]."
- **Directions**: "I need directions to [location]."
